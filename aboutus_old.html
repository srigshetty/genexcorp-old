WELCOM TO Genex Corp. INC

Established in 2008 as "Genex Corp.". has a sound reputation for a systematic and hands-on approach to Human Resources Consulting. In a decade since our inception, we have steadily grown to become one of the India's largest HR services providers and also the Best Service Provider award winner from HP.  

"Genex Corp.". is a value and process driven company entered into private sector and continued its services and operations under the name "Genex Corp." from 2008 and implementing advanced methodologies in staffing and recuiting to serve our clients with a better perspective.

 

Our success as a premier staffing and software solution company is mirrored in the fact that we have placed more than 200 personnel in 31 client locationswhere moat of these being at the GM and above levels.

The spirit of Genex Corp. has been largely defined and embodied by the dedicated and tireless pursuit of excellence by our 37 Principal Consultants, with a mean experience of 12 years at the senior management level. Our Consultants have not only the specialized skills that are services require, but also the ability to understand the bigger picture and visualize the path ahead. We also have a core member staff of 150 and over 1000 deputees on temping mode.

The key to our company's success lies in the quality of service provided. Genex Corp. employs experienced professionals to ensure this quality. Utilizing the latest technologies, our team is skilled in identifying the resources, analyzing alternative solutions, implementing changes and providing on-going expertise to our clients via professional I/T staffing services.

If you value working with a software solution company that is extremely Agile ... thoroughly Service-oriented ... and quite Knowledgeable about every aspect of the employment industry, Genex Corp. is clearly your best choice.

Our company is a blend of dedicated business professionals committed to meeting your company's needs. Our allegiance to this goal and our high quality service guarantees our client's success.

We knew from our first day that solid relationships with both employers and candidates would be essential to success for everyone concerned. And that philosophy has certainly worked: we have doubled our revenue each year, and expect to do even better this year. Our recent agreements with several large clients to supply contract personnel will clearly help us continue that growth record.

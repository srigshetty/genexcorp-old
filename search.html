Shreeji as HP's NSP

Shreeji was incorporated in 1995 with a clear vision of making it a role model by achieving excellence in customer service and we are at our best by becoming a Neutral Service Provider to to the global Organization HP

Oue journy started as NSP to Digital Equipment Corporation (I) Ltd. DEC the then world leader in networking, networked personal computers and super minis had a large installed base in Indian corporate segment.

When Compaq, the no.1 PC maker took over DEC in 1997, our excellent track record in supporting DEC machines qualified us to become Service Provider to new Compaq. Very soon we started supporting some of the top corporate customers through Compaq.

When Compaq merged with Hewlett Packard, again our performance qualified us to become NSP (Neutral Service Provider) of new HP. Today we support many large corporate customers, govt. undertakings and financial institutions through HP.

Now we are associated with HP as Neutral Service Provider with extensive list of Services.
